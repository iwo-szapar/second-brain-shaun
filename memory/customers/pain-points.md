## Pain Point Discovery Questions

### Open-Ended Probes
- "What's the biggest challenge you face with [area]?"
- "Walk me through what happens when [pain situation] occurs."
- "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about [area], what would it be?"

### Specific Probes
- "How much time does your team spend on [pain activity]?"
- "What have you tried to solve [pain]? What worked/didn't work?"
- "What does this problem cost you in [time/money/opportunity]?"

### Validation Questions
- "On a scale of 1-10, how urgent is solving this?"
- "If we could solve [pain], what would that mean for your [goal]?"
- "Who else on your team feels this pain?"
