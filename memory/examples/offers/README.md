# Offer & Proposal Examples

> Your best client-facing materials that demonstrate value positioning and compelling offers.

---

## What to Include Here

### Quality Criteria
Add offers and proposals that:
- **Won the business**: Resulted in signed contracts or agreements
- **Clear positioning**: Articulated value proposition effectively
- **Client-focused**: Addressed specific pain points and outcomes
- **Professional quality**: Well-structured, polished, comprehensive
- **Authentic voice**: Maintained your style while being professional

### Ideal Count
**3-5 different offer types** covering:
- Service proposals (consulting, strategy, etc.)
- Product descriptions
- Pitch decks
- Workshop/training proposals
- Retainer agreements

## What the AI Will Learn

From your offer examples, the AI learns:

### Positioning & Value
- How you articulate what you do (service descriptions)
- How you frame value propositions
- How you differentiate from alternatives
- How you justify pricing/investment

### Structure
- How you organize proposals (sections, flow)
- What you include upfront vs. appendix
- How you balance detail and readability
- How you use formatting for scannability

### Client Communication
- How you address pain points specifically
- How you demonstrate understanding of their situation
- How you build credibility and trust
- How you handle objections preemptively

### Professional Voice
- Maintaining authenticity in formal documents
- Balancing expertise with accessibility
- How you use frameworks to build credibility
- Confidence without arrogance

## Offer Types

### Consulting Proposals
- **Include**: Situation analysis, approach, deliverables, timeline, investment
- **Best for**: Teaching AI your consulting methodology framing

### Product Descriptions
- **Include**: Problem → Solution → Benefits → Features → Pricing
- **Best for**: Teaching AI how you position products

### Pitch Decks
- **Include**: Slide-by-slide content and speaker notes
- **Best for**: Teaching AI your presentation narrative flow

### Workshop/Training Proposals
- **Include**: Learning objectives, agenda, outcomes, logistics
- **Best for**: Teaching AI how you structure educational offerings

## Confidentiality Guidelines

Before adding offers:

1. **Anonymize**: Remove client names, specific details, proprietary info
2. **Get permission**: If including real numbers or detailed approaches
3. **Genericize**: Replace specific details with [Industry], [Client Type]
4. **Focus on structure**: It's the HOW you write offers, not the specific WHAT
