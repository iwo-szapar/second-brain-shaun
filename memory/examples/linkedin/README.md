# LinkedIn Examples

> Your best professional insights that demonstrate accessible expertise and authentic engagement.

---

## What to Include Here

### Quality Criteria
Add LinkedIn posts that demonstrate:
- **Professional authenticity**: Expert but accessible
- **Clear value**: Actionable insights, not just observations
- **Engaging hooks**: First line that stops the scroll
- **Experience-based**: Real examples and stories, not generic advice
- **Strong performance**: High engagement (likes, comments, shares)

### Ideal Count
**5-10 diverse examples** covering:
- Different topics (content, strategy, positioning, consulting, etc.)
- Various structures (story-based, insight-driven, list format, etc.)
- Different hooks and opening styles
- Range of lengths (150-300 word sweet spot)

---

## Naming Convention

Use this format:
```
YYYY-MM-DD-key-topic.md
```

Examples:
- `2025-01-10-content-authenticity-framework.md`
- `2025-02-05-positioning-mistakes-story.md`

## What the AI Will Learn

From your LinkedIn examples, the AI learns:

### Voice & Tone
- Professional but not corporate
- Accessible expertise (not talking down or over-simplifying)
- Authentic POV (clear positions, not fence-sitting)
- Personal without being overly casual

### Structure
- Hook patterns that get attention
- How you tell stories in 2-3 sentences
- Where you place the insight/lesson
- How you craft actionable takeaways
- Optimal paragraph breaks for scannability

### Engagement Patterns
- How you ask questions to invite discussion
- When you use formatting (line breaks, bullets, etc.)
- How you balance teaching with curiosity
- What calls-to-action work for you

### Topic Range
- Your expertise areas (content, strategy, consulting, etc.)
- How you connect different topics to frameworks
- What angles resonate with your audience
- How you vary content while staying on-brand
