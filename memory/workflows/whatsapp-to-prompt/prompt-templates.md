# WhatsApp Prompt Templates

## Purpose
Pre-built message templates for common tasks. Copy, customize, and send via WhatsApp.

---

## Template Structure

### Basic Template
```
[Task Type]: [Your Request]
```

### Advanced Template
```
[Task Type]: [Your Request]
Context: [Background info]
Tone: [Professional/Casual/Formal]
Length: [Word count or Short/Medium/Long]
Format: [Email/List/Paragraph/etc.]
```

## Customization Guide

### Personalizing Templates

1. **Replace Placeholders**: [Company Name], [Topic], etc.
2. **Add Your Context**: Include relevant background
3. **Specify Output**: Length, format, tone preferences
4. **Include Examples**: Show what good looks like
5. **Set Constraints**: Word limits, must-include items

### Example Customization

**Template**:
```
Draft Email: [Role Title] at [Company Name]
Context: [Your background]
Tone: Professional but warm
Length: 150-200 words
```

**Customized**:
```
Draft Email: VP of Customer Success at DataFlow Inc
Context: 10 years CS experience in B2B SaaS, scaled teams from 5 to 50+, saw their recent blog post about customer health scoring which aligns with my expertise
Tone: Professional but warm (6 on 1-10 scale)
Length: 180 words max
Include: Reference to their health scoring article, mention my scaling experience
Avoid: Overly formal language, generic enthusiasm
```

## Template Testing

### Test Your Template

1. Send to WhatsApp
2. Review AI output
3. Rate quality (1-10)
4. Identify what to improve
5. Refine template
6. Test again

### Quality Checklist

After receiving response:
- [ ] Matches requested tone?
- [ ] Correct length?
- [ ] Specific enough (not generic)?
- [ ] Accurate facts (no hallucinations)?
- [ ] Usable with minimal editing?

## Advanced Techniques

### Multi-Part Requests

For complex tasks, break into parts:

**Part 1**: Research
```
Research: XYZ Company
Focus: Recent news, culture signals, product details
Length: 200 words, bullet points
```

**Part 2**: Draft (send after reviewing research)
```
Draft Email: Marketing Manager at XYZ Company
Context: [Include key facts from research]
Tone: Professional but warm
Length: 150 words
```

### Iterative Refinement

**First Pass**:
```
Outline: Blog post about CX automation
Audience: SaaS founders
Length: 5 sections
```

**Second Pass** (after reviewing outline):
```
Expand: Section 3 from the CX automation outline
Add: Real-world examples, specific tools
Length: 300 words
Tone: Practical and actionable
```
